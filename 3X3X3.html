The White Cross

The first thing to keep in mind is to keep the White face at top .Also,it is always recommended to start with the white face as it is the brightest of the lot Intermediate and fast Cubers keep the white face in bottom . So once , you start getting a grip on the cube and start solving the cube

In a 3X3x3 cube , all the faces are fixed. Yellow will be opposite to white . White face will be adjacent to blue , orange , green and red. At later stages , if your remember which face is adjacent to which one, this would be benificial, but now it is not required.

For a normal cube , it goes like this

- White and Yellow are opposites.

- Red and Orange are opposites.

- Green and Blue are opposites.

There is no algorithm required to solve the white cross . You have to place all the white edges corresponding to the adjacent faces .

Like , an edge piece of colors white and blue should be placed in such a way that the white part is on the face whose center is white and blue side of the edge should be placed where the face has blue center piece.

Note :The Center piece of the face determines what will be the color of the face

Inverted Edges

You can do it either ways , by using the right or the left side . The Algorithm will be similar

The case we are taking is where the adjacent right face already has the edge placed. If the edge is not there on the right side

A shorter approach will be taken which you can figure out yourself once you start applying this algorithm

Followings steps will ensure you get the edge the way you want

F Ri D R F F

This step is easy so practice it a few times and then head on to the Next Step

WHITE LAYER

Now for the white layer you have to insert the corner pieces . First thing you need to do is to put the white face at the bottom

Which means the yellow face should be at top

A corner piece has three colors , so insert the corner piece in such a way that all the faces of the corner matches the faces of the cube

For this three cases will arive . Don't worry , They can be completed in 3 to 4 moves

Note :To make things easier the corner piece will be placed at the right side of the cube and the algorithms will be put accordingly

White facing Upwards

This case can be easily solved . Apply First or Second Algorithm

If you apply Ui Fi U F , then apply R U

SECOND LAYER

You have reached this stage means that you are comfortable in solving the white layer

This is the place where the algorithm will get a little longer , but dont worry , It uses two faces at a time , so remembering this wont be a problem

Note :To Explain this we have taken Red as the FRONT FACE

For the second layer , there are three cases

- Slotting the edge which involves front and the right face

- Slotting the edge which involves front and the left face

- Piece is already sorted but inverted

Slotting Between Front and the Right Face

You can see that the red green edge has to be brought in the second layer at the front-right edge slot

The Algorithm that we need to use for this is

U R Ui Ri Ui Fi U F
U R Ui Ri
Ui Fi U F

Slotting Between Front and the Left Face

You can see that the red blue edge has to be brought in the second layer at the front-left edge slot

Note :Red will remain the front face . To illustrate slotting of the edge in the front-left edge slot , right face is taken as front face i.e Red

The Algorithm that we need to use for this is

Ui Li U L U F Ui Fi
Ui Li U L
U F Ui Fi

Inverted Edge

For this case You would have to apply the algorithm twice depending upon the case

Inverted at the front-right slot

U R Ui Ri Ui Fi U F U U U R Ui Ri Ui Fi U F

Inverted at the front-left slot

Ui Li U L U F Ui Fi U U Ui Li U L U F Ui Fi
